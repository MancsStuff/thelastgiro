2. What Data We Collect and Why

When you sign up to our email list:

When you buy a course or membership:

  • Legal basis: Our contract with you

When you visit our website:

When you book a call:

  • Legal basis: Your consent

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@thelastgiro.com with the subject line matching your request. We will respond within 30 days.
