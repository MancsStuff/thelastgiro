The UC Taper Rate — Plain English

If you're on Universal Credit, this is the thing that stops most people before they start. Let's deal with it now — with real numbers.

The taper rate is 55%. For every £1 of profit you earn over your work allowance, DWP reduces your UC by 55p. You keep 45p. It always pays more to earn than not to earn.

But here's what most people don't know: you report profit — not income — to DWP.

Profit = Income minus allowable business expenses. Every legitimate expense reduces what DWP sees. Your phone bill, your subscriptions, your equipment — all deductible. Module 11 covers every expense in detail.
