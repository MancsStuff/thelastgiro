UC, Benefits & Earning

The Taper Rate — Plain English

The UC taper rate is 55%. For every £1 of profit you earn over your work allowance, DWP reduces your UC by 55p. You keep 45p.

Monthly profitUC reductionReal gain
£100-£55+£45 better off
£200-£110+£90 better off
£500-£275+£225 better off
£1,000-£550+£450 better off

You are always better off earning than not earning. Every single time.

The Expenses Trick

You report profit — not income — to DWP. Profit = income minus allowable expenses. Every expense you claim reduces the number DWP uses. Module 11 covers every expense you can legally claim.

⚠ Do This Before Your First Sale

Log into your UC journal online and add a note: "I am starting a self-employed business. I will report all earnings in my monthly assessment period." Screenshot it. That protects you if DWP ever questions when you started.

The Minimum Income Floor — Know This

After 12 months of self-employment, DWP may apply the Minimum Income Floor — an assumed earnings level. It does NOT apply in your first 12 months. If you have a health condition limiting your capacity for work, it may not apply at all. Ask your work coach before month 12.
