Products to Create & Launch

One free offer to build trust. One paid offer to earn money. One recurring offer for stability. This module also covers the single most important rule before you name anything.

⚠ Critical Rule — Check the Name BEFORE You Build Anything

Before you name your business, your course, your service — check it is available everywhere. Building something under a name that's already taken costs hours of wasted work and forces you to start again.

Check these in order: Google search, Companies House (companieshouse.gov.uk), Teachable, Skool, Gumroad, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook. If it exists anywhere — pick a different name.

This lesson was learned the hard way. Don't repeat it.

The Three-Layer Business

  • Layer 1 — Free offer — Solves a small real problem at zero cost. Builds trust. Gets you in front of people. Takes 20–30 minutes using AI.
  • Layer 2 — Paid offer — One clear thing. One clear price. One clear outcome. Simple enough to explain in one sentence.
  • Layer 3 — Recurring offer — Monthly retainer or subscription. Even 3 clients at £50/month = £150 guaranteed every month. This builds stability.

Naming Your Offer

Good offer name formula

[Number or frequency] + [Specific deliverable] + [For who] + [Outcome]

"10 social posts a month for local tradespeople — so your phone keeps ringing even when you're on a job."

Your First Free Sample

  • One free social media post — designed and written, ready to publish. 20 minutes with AI.
  • A free rewrite of their bio — 30 minutes, immediately shows the difference.
  • One free graphic — business card, flyer header, social banner. 20 minutes in Microsoft Designer.
The free sample rule

Do the free sample. Get their reaction. If they're happy, ask: "I charge £[price] a month for ongoing work like this — would that be useful?" That's the entire sales process.

AI Prompt — Design Your Three Offers

"I help [your ideal client] with [your skill]. Design three offers for me: (1) a free sample I can deliver in under 30 minutes using AI, (2) a core paid offer priced between £[low] and £[high] with a specific deliverable and one-sentence description, (3) a monthly recurring offer with everything included and a monthly price."

✓ Before You Move to the Next Module — Confirm These
  • I have checked my business name is available on Google, Companies House, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, Gumroad
  • I have written my free sample offer — what it is and how long it takes
  • I have written my paid offer — what it is, what it costs, one sentence description
  • I have written my recurring offer — what's included and the monthly price
