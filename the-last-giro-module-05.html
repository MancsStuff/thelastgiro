The Funnel & Client Journey

A funnel is the path someone takes from not knowing you exist to paying you money. Four steps. Simple. This module maps it out for your specific business.

The Four Steps

  • Step 1 — Awareness — They see you. TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, word of mouth, Google. Pick ONE platform first. Do it well. Add more later.
  • Step 2 — Interest — They check you out. Landing page, free sample, link in bio. Give them something before you ask for anything.
  • Step 3 — Decision — They reach out. DM, WhatsApp, email. You send your pitch. You have a 15-minute conversation. No hard sell — just a conversation about whether you can help.
  • Step 4 — Action — They pay you. PayPal, Stripe, bank transfer. Always get paid before you start work. No exceptions.
Common mistake — too many platforms at once

Starting on TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and Threads simultaneously means doing all of them badly. Pick one. Build it properly. Add a second only when the first is working.

Platform by Platform

TikTok

Best for reaching people who don't know you yet. High reach, no followers needed to start. Post value first — tips, tool demos, honest content. Your ideal clients are on here even if they don't seem like a TikTok audience.

Facebook & Facebook Groups

Best for local businesses and community reach. Join groups where your ideal clients are. Search "[your town] business" or "[your trade] UK". Be helpful first. Sell later. Direct message works very well here.

Instagram

Best for visual services — design, photography, food, beauty. Before and afters. Behind the scenes. Local hashtags.

Word of Mouth

The most underrated channel. Tell 10 people what you're doing. One referral from a trusted source is worth 100 cold messages.

The DM Script That Works

Copy this exactly

"Hi [name] — I came across your [page/post/business] and noticed [specific observation]. I help [type of business] with [your service]. Happy to do a free sample this week if it'd be useful — no obligation."

AI Prompt — Map Your Funnel

"Map out a 4-step customer funnel for a [business type] targeting [your ideal client]. For each step tell me: which platform or channel, what content or action happens there, what the call to action is, and what the most common reason people drop off at that stage is and how to fix it."
