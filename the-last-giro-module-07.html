Your Daily Workflow

  • Morning (15 mins) — Open Super Whisper. Speak your post idea. Claude or DeepSeek turns it into a polished caption. Post it.
  • Client work (30–60 mins) — Claude for writing, Microsoft Designer for graphics, Agent Opus Clip for video. Deliver via WhatsApp.
  • Engagement (10 mins) — Reply personally to every comment and DM. First 50 replies matter most for reach.

Total: under 90 minutes a day to run a professional content and service business.

Total monthly cost using ALL these tools

Super Whisper ~£8 + Adobe Express if upgraded £9 = under £20/month maximum. Everything else is free. A full-time member of staff costs £1,500+ per month. You're replacing that team for the price of a takeaway.
