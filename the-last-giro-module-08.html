Your Landing Page

One page. One job. Turns a visitor into a lead or a buyer. This module gives you the structure, the copy, and step-by-step instructions to build it today — free.

⚠ Laptop Required for This Module

Building a website on Carrd or Google Sites is much harder on a phone. If possible, use a laptop for this module. Library computers are free and work perfectly.

If you only have a phone — use a pinned Facebook post as your landing page for now. It works. Don't let the website stop you starting.

The Six-Part Page Structure

  • Headline — Who you help, what you do, where. One sentence. "I help Manchester tradespeople get more enquiries using social media — from £75/month."
  • The Problem — Their pain in their words. "You're too busy on the tools to keep your Facebook going. Meanwhile your competitors look active every day."
  • The Solution — What you do about it. Plain English. No jargon.
  • Social Proof — One testimonial. Even one sentence from a freebie client.
  • The Offer — What they get, what it costs, what happens when they sign up.
  • Call to Action — One button. One action. "Message me on WhatsApp" or "Email me today." Never two options.

Free Tools to Build Your Page

  • Carrd.co — Simplest. Free tier. Professional. Live in 20 minutes. — carrd.co
  • Google Sites — Completely free. Easy to update. — sites.google.com
  • GitHub Pages — Free hosting. What The Last Giro website runs on. Needs laptop.
  • Pinned Facebook Post — Zero cost. One post pinned to top of your page with your offer. Many people start here and earn their first £100 before building a proper page.

Carrd Walkthrough — 20 Minutes to Live

  • Go to carrd.co — tap Start Building
  • Choose any template — you'll replace all the text anyway
  • Replace the headline with your one-sentence offer
  • Add your problem paragraph, solution paragraph, testimonial
  • Add one button linked to your WhatsApp: wa.me/44YOURNUMBER (replace YOURNUMBER with your number, no spaces, no leading 0)
  • Tap Publish — it's live. Share the link in your social bios immediately.
If you're building on GitHub Pages

GitHub requires a laptop. DNS changes (pointing your domain to GitHub) take 10–30 minutes to propagate — do not panic if the site doesn't appear immediately.

Enforce HTTPS only activates after DNS has fully propagated. If the checkbox is greyed out — wait 30 minutes and try again.

For DNS records: you need 4 x A records (185.199.108.153, 185.199.109.153, 185.199.110.153, 185.199.111.153) and 1 CNAME (www pointing to yourusername.github.io). Delete any existing ALIAS record first or it will conflict.

AI Prompt — Write Your Landing Page Copy

"Write a landing page for my [business type] targeting [ideal client]. My offer is [your offer] at £[price] per month. Structure: headline, problem paragraph, solution paragraph, testimonial placeholder, offer details, one call-to-action button. Under 250 words. Plain and direct."
