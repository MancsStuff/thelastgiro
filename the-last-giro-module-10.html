Your One-Page Pitch

When someone asks what you do — you send them this. One page. No waffle. Generated by AI in 5 minutes. This is the document that turns conversations into clients.

The Eight Elements

  • Your name and what you do — one sentence, plain English, zero jargon
  • Who you help — specific type of person or business, not "everyone"
  • The problem you solve — in their words, not yours
  • What you offer — 1 or 2 services, simply explained
  • What it costs — be upfront, saves everyone's time and builds trust immediately
  • What they get as an outcome — not features. Results.
  • One testimonial — even one sentence doubles conversion rate
  • How to start — one clear next step. "Message me on WhatsApp for a free 15-minute chat."

Three Templates — Pick Yours

Template 1 — Service Business (social media, writing, design)

"Hi, I'm [name]. I help [local businesses / tradespeople] in [area] with [service] so they can [outcome].

I [what you do specifically] for £[price]/month. No contracts. Cancel any time.

[Client name] said: '[one line testimonial]'

Happy to do one free [sample] so you can see the quality first. Message me on WhatsApp: [number]."

Template 2 — Freelance / one-off work

"I'm [name]. I help [client type] with [specific task] using AI tools. Whether it's [example 1], [example 2], or [example 3] — I deliver fast, professional results.

One-off projects from £[price]. Most turned around within 48 hours.

Free sample available. Email: [email] or WhatsApp: [number]."

Template 3 — Course or knowledge business

"I'm [name]. I teach [type of person] how to [main outcome] using [method / tools].

[Course name] — [what's included] — £[price]. Everything you need to [result], step by step.

Module 1 is completely free — no email required. Start there and judge for yourself.

[Link to free module]"

Build It in Microsoft Designer

  • Generate your copy using the AI prompt below
  • Open Microsoft Designer — search for "one-page flyer" or "services brochure" template
  • Paste your copy in — adjust colours to match your brand
  • Export as PDF
  • Send on WhatsApp. Pin to social profiles. Use everywhere.
AI Prompt — Write Your Pitch Right Now

"Write a one-page pitch for my business. I do [service] for [client type] in [area or online]. My offer is [what's included] for £[price]. Main outcome: [result]. Use this structure: name and what I do, who I help, the problem, the offer, the price, the outcome, a testimonial placeholder, call to action. Under 200 words. Direct. No corporate speak."
